Privacy Policy

YouTube Milliseconds Timer  ·  Last updated: April 2026

What data we collect

None. The extension does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Local storage

The extension stores the following data exclusively on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API:

  • User settings — milliseconds toggle, interval timer toggle
  • Watch time statistics — total time and per-day breakdown

This data never leaves your browser and is not accessible to anyone other than you.

Clipboard

The extension writes to the clipboard only when you explicitly click a copy button (timestamp or interval delta). It never reads from the clipboard and never accesses it automatically.

Third-party services

None. No analytics, no tracking pixels, no external requests of any kind.

Permissions

  • storage — to save your settings and watch time statistics locally
  • clipboardWrite — to copy timestamps when you click the copy button
  • youtube.com — to inject the extension UI into YouTube pages

Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be published at this URL and the "Last updated" date above will change.
