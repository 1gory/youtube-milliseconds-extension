YouTube Milliseconds Timer

Millisecond-accurate timestamps, interval timer A→B, one-click copy, and a watch time stats dashboard — all inside YouTube's native player.

⏱

Millisecond timestamps

Time displays in M:SS.mmm format directly in the player — no overlays, no clutter. Toggle on/off anytime.

📋

One-click copy

A copy button next to the timestamp lets you grab the exact moment instantly — great for notes, subtitles, or editing.

↔️

Interval timer A→B

Mark two points with [ and ], see the exact delta down to the millisecond, copy it with one click.

📊

Watch time stats

Total time, today, daily average, 7-day bar chart, and a monthly calendar heatmap — all stored locally on your device.
