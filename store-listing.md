Adds millisecond precision to YouTube video timestamps, copy any moment with one click, and track total watch time


FULL DESCRIPTION
----------------
YouTube Milliseconds Timer adds millisecond precision to YouTube's video player — perfect for video editors, researchers, educators, and anyone who needs to pinpoint exact moments.

FEATURES

Millisecond-accurate timestamps
Displays time in MM:SS.mmm format (e.g. 1:23:45.678) directly in the YouTube player. No overlays, no clutter — replaces the native timestamp seamlessly.

One-click timestamp copy
A small copy button appears next to the time. Click it to instantly copy the exact timestamp to your clipboard — great for notes, subtitles, video editing, or sharing a specific moment.

Watch time tracker
Tracks your total YouTube viewing time across all sessions, including Shorts. See your stats in the extension popup. Resets anytime.

Toggle milliseconds on/off
Prefer the standard look? Switch milliseconds off in one click from the popup — the copy button stays available.

PRIVACY

No accounts. No tracking. No data leaves your device. All stats are stored locally in your browser.


PRIVACY TAB
-----------
Single purpose:
Displays millisecond-precision timestamps on YouTube videos and allows copying them

Justification for storage:
Stores user settings and watch time statistics locally on the device. No data leaves the device.

Justification for host permissions (youtube.com):
The extension injects a content script into YouTube pages to access the video player's time display and modify it with millisecond precision. Access is limited to youtube.com only.

Justification for clipboardWrite:
Copies the current video timestamp to clipboard only when the user explicitly clicks the copy button. The extension never accesses the clipboard without user action.

Remote code: No

Data collection: No
